FAQ

How will I get there?▶
Newland Hall is situated 4 miles west of Chelmsford on the A1060. We are on the left (coming from Chelmsford) and sign-posted. Continue down the drive to Reception. Short cab journey from Chelmsford Station. Bus route No. 59 from Chelmsford, bus stops at the end of the drive. Sat Nav Postcode: CM1 4LH
Is there parking at the venue?▶
Yes, there is.
What time should I arrive?▶
Our ceremony will begin promptly at 2.30pm, we are asking guests to arrive 30 minutes before. This will allow plenty of time to park, get a drink and take your seats.
What should I wear?▶
It is important to us that our guests are comfortable with what they are wearing, we are going with smart/casual attire.
Can I bring a guest/date?▶
If you received a plus one or your guest’s name is on the invitation, then yes, otherwise we would love to keep our wedding as an intimate event with close family and friends.
